Our Purpose

Furthering the Game,
Building Friendships

The purpose of the Golden Hill Golf Club is to further the game of golf among its members and within the community, to promote good fellowship, and to foster better relationships among its members and friends.

Since its establishment in 1968, the GHGC has remained open and committed to all players interested in making new friends and improving their game. We welcome players of all levels and backgrounds.

Our Story

Born from a "Who Needs Them" Spirit

The Golden Hill Golf Club was formed in response to some of the charter members being turned down by another club. Fortified by a few drinks, they decided to start their own — and the rest is history.

1968

GHGC is founded with a rebellious spirit and a love for the game. The founding group believed that fun at the 19th hole makes up for the other eighteen.

Year One

Before the end of the first year, the club grew to 60 members. By the following year, membership expanded to 90 with people on the waiting list.

1970

Two years after GHGC was formed, the Federation of Chinese Golfers (now Association of International Golf Clubs) was established. GHGC was one of the founding members.

Today

GHGC is an NCGA Associate Golf Club adhering to USGA rules and handicap system. Members actively participate in AIGC Federation Tournaments and have fielded many winners.
